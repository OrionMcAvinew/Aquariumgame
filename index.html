🐠 How to Play

  • Buy fish from the Wholesale Market, then click a tank to place them.
  • Keep tanks healthy: feed your fish and clean the water. Hungry fish in dirty tanks get sick and can die.
  • Serve customers: shoppers appear at the top wanting a specific fish. Click them to make the sale before their patience runs out.
  • Reputation (⭐) grows with every sale and unlocks rarer, more valuable species. Missed customers and dead fish hurt it.
  • Upgrades: filters slow grime, auto-feeders keep bellies full, and decorations let you charge 20% more. Buy extra tanks to stock more species.
  • Rent is due daily — the more tanks you own, the higher it gets. Keep selling!
